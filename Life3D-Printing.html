Interest in a amazing technology

I’m really not sure where my interest of 3-D printing came from aside from the idea that I could legitimately make anything I could think of and it started there that I got my first 3-D printer for about two or $300 and it started an obsession with making little parts for projects I was working on, or maybe Just something that’s somewhat useful but what I’ll be showing is a solution to a problem that I was having. When I started school, I realized I enjoy quiet, and in my household it’s often not quiet so my solution was to hang up soundproofing foam. I’ve personally didn’t want to damage the walls and looking for a easy to use product I was left, empty and with the obvious frustration at most people seem to spray the wall with glue and put soundproofing foam on that I would sit out to make a solution that would be accessible to someone who only wanted to use command strips and minimal effort. While minimal effort was not achieved something that could work while only using command strips was possible and I now use the solution in my room to cover my door in soundproofing foam.
